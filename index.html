The Problem We're Solving

Increased Injury Risk

Shoes with limited grip can cause slips and sprains. Damaged frames can lead to wrist pain or tennis elbow. Playing safe means using safe equipment.

Compromised Performance

Worn-out gear like tennis balls with bad bounce, damaged racket frames, and unstable shoes directly hinders performance and leads to unpredictable play.

Lack of Awareness

Most players simply don't know when their equipment is past its prime. They lack a convenient, data-driven way to assess gear health.

Market Size Calculation Assumptions

Our market size is calculated using a top-down approach based on the following assumptions:

  • Total Addressable Market (TAM): Represents the total estimated number of recreational players for each sport in India.
  • Serviceable Available Market (SAM): We assume we can reach and attract 30% of the TAM who would be interested in an app like ours.
    Formula: SAM = TAM * 30%
  • Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM): Our initial realistic target is to capture 15% of the SAM as our active user base in the early years.
    Formula: SOM = SAM * 15%
